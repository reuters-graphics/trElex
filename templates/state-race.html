<%= gettext("Democratic Caucuses")%>

The Iowa Democratic Party reported alignment numbers in 2020 in addition to State Delegate Equivalents (SDEs). “Final alignment” represents the number of people who caucused for each candidate in the final round after caucus-goers who lined up with candidates who failed to reach at least 15 percent in the first round are allowed to change groups.

Candidate order and percentage are based on State Delegate Equivalents (SDEs), which are the estimated number of state convention delegates each candidate receives based on final alignment. Results are reported by Edison Research for the National Election Pool, which calls the winner.

<%= gettext("Republican Caucuses")%>

The Republican Party conducts a straw poll at every precinct caucus. The results of the straw poll are the only numbers reported for the Republican Caucuses.
